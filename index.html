dablo automobile financial indicators analysis using excel

This project entails a four-year simulation of Dablo Motors, examining the forecasted and actual results of key performance indicators (KPIs) and the effects of decisions made. The analysis includes financial indicators to assess the company's performance over the years. Throughout the simulation, Diablo's performance was evaluated against forecasted projections, revealing significant insights into sales growth, customer satisfaction, market share, and financial metrics. Notably, the comparison highlighted areas where the company exceeded expectations and identified opportunities for improvement. Key financial indicators such as revenue, profit, ROCE, ROI and financial ratios were analyzed to gauge Diablo's financial health and operational efficiency. The simulation provided valuable insights into the company's ability to adapt to market changes, make informed decisions, and achieve sustainable growth. These findings offer a robust framework for real-world strategic planning, emphasizing the importance of data-driven decision-making and proactive management.
